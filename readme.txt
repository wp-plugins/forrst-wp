Forrst WP adds a Forrst Widget for you to display up to 10 of a user's latest Forrst posts they have made

== Description ==

Forrst WP is a WordPress plugin by [iGeek](http://www.igeek.co.za/ "iGeek") that allows you to place a widget on your WordPress site that enables you to show your latest posts on Forrst.

Forrst WP allows you full control over what user's posts you would like to display and how many posts to display at a time.


== Installation ==

= Installation =

1. Upload `forrst-wp` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to Appearance > Widgets and drag the Forrst widget to where you want to display it.
1. Enter the desired Forrst username's posts you would like to display in your widget and the amount of posts to display.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Forrst WP won't display more than 10 posts =

Due to the Forrst API being in its infant stages at the moment you can not retrieve more than 10 posts per Forrst user at a time
